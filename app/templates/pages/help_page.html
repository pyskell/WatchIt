To register an address for tracking with WatchIt you'll need to send a transaction on the Ethereum Classic blockchain.

  1. 1. Open up your wallet as you normally would to send ETC.
  2. 2. Create a transaction going to address:
  3. 3. You can leave the amount to send as zero. If you do send any ETC it will be considered a donation.
  4. 4. Copy/Paste your "Public UUID" to the Input Data/Additional Data box: Note: You may need to select an "advanced" option in your wallet to do this
  5. 5. Send your transaction

Once a wallet is registered we'll send you an email whenever its balance increases or decreases. Email alerts are limited to a maximum of once every {{email_time_limit.seconds // 60}} minutes.

Donations are appreciated, they'd help me add functionality (ie. more coins) to this site and make it prettier. Maybe even mobile alerts in the future. Also website hosting and emails ain't free ya know.

We CAN NOT support monitoring wallets on exchanges (ie. Poloniex, Kraken, Bitfinex, Bittrex, Coinbase, Gemini, etc.). This is due to exchange wallets operating differently from normal wallets.
