To register an address for tracking with WatchIt you'll need to send a transaction on the Ethereum Classic blockchain.

  1. 1. Open up your wallet as you normally would to send ETC.
  2. 2. Create a transaction going to address:
  3. 3. You can leave the amount to send as zero, any amount you enter will be considered a donation
  4. 4. Copy/Paste your "Public UUID" to the Input Data/Additional Data box:
  5. 5. Send your transaction

Please note: Donations are appreciated, they'd help me add functionality (ie. more coins) to this site and make it prettier. Maybe even mobile alerts in the future.

We can not support monitoring wallets on exchanges (ie. Poloniex, Kraken, Bitfinex, Bittrex, Coinbase, Gemini, etc.). This is due to exchange wallets operating differently from normal wallets.
